About Me

My name is Jared and music is my passion. I am a music producer and DJ going by the moniker gnarbear. I have performed live in Mallorca, for company events, and various private parties.

As a DJ, I play gigs based on the theme or that fits the occasion, ranging from chill low-fi electronic music to dancy pop remixes. And as a producer, I enjoy creating chilltronic music with hip-hop infused beats.
